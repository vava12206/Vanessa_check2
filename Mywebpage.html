Introduction to health

According to World Health Organization (WHO), Health is a way of maintaining physical, mental and social well-being of people. A healthful lifestyle provide the means to lead a fill life with purpose and meaning.

Good health is central to handling stress and living a longer, more active life. In this article, we explain the meaning of good health, the types of health a person needs to consider, and how to preserve good health.Physical health, Mental healthand Social health

Factors of good health

1.Environmental factor

The environment we live in has a big impact on our health. Our environment can cause illness in person who has an increased genetic risk of a certain diseases.

  • The following factors may have a more significant impact on health:
    • Wealth
    • Where a person lives
    • Level of education

    • 2.Genetic factor

    A person is born with different genes. Some people inherit genes from their parents that can cause them a risk of some health condition.
